Air Fryer Pork Chops

Description

These pork chops prove why cooking with an air fryer is the way to go. This short and easy recipe is sure to impress friends, family, and anyone lucky enough to get a taste of what youre cooking.

Ingrediants

  • 1/2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 boneless Pork Chops
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the airfryer to 380 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper in a flat shallow dish; mix well.
  3. Coat each pork chop with olive oil. Dredge both sides of each chop in the Parmesan mixture and set on a plate.
  4. Place 2 chops in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes; flipping halfway through cook time. Transfer to a cutting board and let rest for 5 minutes. Reapeat with remaining chops.
  5. Served hot and enjoy!
