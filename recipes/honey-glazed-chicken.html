Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This honey chicken takes just 20 minutes to make. This recipe is a family favorite and is a must if you want to impress the friends with a fast and delicious meal. Its light, tangy, and fun to make. Try it out!

Ingrediants

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
