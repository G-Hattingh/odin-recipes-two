Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

This honey chicken takes just 20 minutes to make. This recipe is a family favorite and is a must if you want to impress the friends with a fast and delicious meal. Its light, tangy, and fun to make. Try it out!

Ingrediants

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingrediants.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oul until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
