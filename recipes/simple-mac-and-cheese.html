Simple Mac and Cheese

Description

This homemade alternative to boxed mac and cheese is easy, quick, and delicious. This recipe uses staple ingredients that you know and love and is ready to eat in just 25 minutes. Overpriced designer mac and cheese usually costs forty or fifty dollars, take longer to prepare, and dont always taste the best. This recipe is the best of both worlds. All you need is a pot, boiling water, a roux mixture, and your macaroni.
