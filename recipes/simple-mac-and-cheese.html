Simple Mac and Cheese

Description

This homemade alternative to boxed mac and cheese is easy, quick, and delicious. This recipe uses staple ingredients that you know and love and is ready to eat in just 25 minutes. Overpriced designer mac and cheese usually costs forty or fifty dollars, take longer to prepare, and dont always taste the best. This recipe is the best of both worlds. All you need is a pot, boiling water, a roux mixture, and your macaroni.

Ingrediants

  • 1 box of elbow Macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Chedder cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni until al dente, about 8 minutes.
  2. While the macaroni is cooking, go ahead and start on the roux. A roux is a thickening agent made of one part fat and one part flour that makes the base of this creamy mac and cheese. To make the roux, start by melting butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth. Slowly pour in milk and stir until smooth and bubbling. Be careful to not let the milk burn.
  3. Finally, add the most crucial step: Add cheese! Slowly stir in Chedder cheese until smooth and melted. We recommend you grate your own cheese because pre-shredded cheese wont incorporate into the mixture as well as block cheese.Once the macaroni is finished cooking drain and stir into cheeae sauce until coated
